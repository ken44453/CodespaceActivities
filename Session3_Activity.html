Cats

Siamese Cat

The Siamese cat is known for its striking blue almond-shaped eyes and short coat.

Did you know? Siamese cats are known for being vocal and affectionate.

  • Origin: Thailand
  • Lifespan: 15-20 years
  • Weight: 6-10 pounds

Persian Cat

Persian cats have long, luxurious coats and a sweet, calm temperament.

Did you know? Persian cats require regular grooming to keep their fur in good condition.

  • Origin: Persia (modern-day Iran)
  • Lifespan: 12-16 years
  • Weight: 7-12 pounds

Maine Coon

Maine Coon cats are one of the largest domesticated cat breeds, known for their friendly nature and tufted ears.

Did you know? Maine Coon cats are often referred to as "gentle giants."

  • Origin: United States (Maine)
  • Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Weight: 10-25 pounds

Ragdoll Cat

Ragdoll cats are known for their large size, striking blue eyes, and their tendency to go limp when held.

Did you know? Ragdoll cats are gentle and affectionate, making them great companions.

  • Origin: United States (California)
  • Lifespan: 12-17 years
  • Weight: 12-20 pounds

British Shorthair

The British Shorthair is known for its round face, dense coat, and stocky build, giving it a charming teddy bear appearance.

Did you know? British Shorthairs are easygoing and make wonderful indoor companions.

  • Origin: United Kingdom
  • Lifespan: 12-20 years
  • Weight: 9-18 pounds
